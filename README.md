## ALS
### Takeuchi, Brain, 2025. snRNA-seq
* Paper : https://academic.oup.com/brain/advance-article/doi/10.1093/brain/awaf426/8321227
* Dataset : https://cellxgene.cziscience.com/collections/0986e4cd-7a58-405d-9b91-4b199bb4124e
* Processed snRNA-seq and snATAC-seq data for ALS and controls in primary motor cortex and spinal cord [6 ALS and 6 controls]
* Lymphocytes in cluster in lumbar spinal chord dataset
### Tuddenham, Acta Neuropathologica, 2025. scRNA-seq 
* Paper : https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00401-025-02913-3#data-availability
* Dataset : https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE204704
* scRNA-seq from brain and spinal chord [9 ALS (5M, 4F)]
* contains T cell cluster

### Zelic, Immunity, 2025. snRNA-seq and bulk 
* Paper: https://doi-org.libproxy.lib.unc.edu/10.1016/j.immuni.2025.02.024
* Dataset exploration tool: https://talk2data.bioturing.com/sanofibrowser/
* bulk and single-nucleus RNA-seq (snRNA-seq) on cervical spinal cords from six sporadic and two familial ALS cases and four age- and sex-matched controls [8 ALS, 4 controls]
* Bulk RNA seq: https://www-ncbi-nlm-nih-gov.libproxy.lib.unc.edu/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE287256
* snRNA-seq : https://www-ncbi-nlm-nih-gov.libproxy.lib.unc.edu/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE287257
* has lymphocyte cluster
